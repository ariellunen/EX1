Money Heist



A criminal mastermind who goes by "The Professor" has a plan to pull off the biggest heist in recorded history - to print billions of euros
in the Royal Mint of Spain. He recruits eight people with certain abilities and who have nothing to lose.

Best Quotes from the series

  1. "After all, what is more human than the fight for survival?"
  2. In this world, everything is governed by balance. There is what you stand to gain and what you stand to lose.
    And when you think you have got nothing to lose, you become overconfident.
